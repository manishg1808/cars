Things to See & Do in Orlando, Florida

From world-famous theme parks to premium shopping and unforgettable marine experiences, Orlando offers something for every traveler.

Orlando International Premium Outlets

With savings of 25%-65% every day and the mix of designer and exclusive-to-market retail makes for a must see shopping experience for both international and local shoppers.

Walt Disney World Resort

This world-famous resort consists of four Theme Parks, two Water Parks, over 25 Resort hotel properties, and a variety of additional entertainment offerings.

SeaWorld

Immerse yourself in wonder at SeaWorld Orlando, where the aquatic world comes alive with major attractions, marine encounters, and thrilling rides.

The Wizarding World of Harry Potter at Universal Orlando Resort

Step inside a world where magic is real. Visit the iconic Hogwarts castle and explore Hogsmeade village inside Universal's Islands of Adventure.

Universal Studios Florida

Discovery Cove

Enjoy a one-of-a-kind day where you can interact with dolphins, feed tropical birds, meet otters, and explore the Grand Reef in a relaxed setting.
